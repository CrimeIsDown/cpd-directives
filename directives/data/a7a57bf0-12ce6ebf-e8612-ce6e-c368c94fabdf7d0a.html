Chicago Police DepartmentUniform and Property U04-01-04
Summer Detail Uniform
Issue Date:12 December 2013Effective Date:20 December 2013
Rescinds:25 November 1998 Version, U04-01-12
Index Category:Uniform and Personal Equipment
I.Purpose
This directive describes the approved summer detail uniform and provides the detailed list of items for the summer detail uniform.
II.Policy
  • A.Members are authorized to wear the optional summer detail uniform:
    • 1.from 01 June through 30 September or when the temperature is expected to exceed 80 degrees; and
    • 2.when assigned or detailed to marathons, outdoor concerts, musical festivals, parades, outdoor neighborhood events and festivals, outdoor sporting events, lakefront events, and
    • 3.the event the member has been assigned or detailed to has the summer detail uniform approved in writing within an Operational Order issued by the Special Events Unit and approved by the First Deputy Superintendent, or
    • 4.if not part of an Operational Order issued by the Special Events Unit and approved by the First Deputy Superintendent, then approved in writing by the appropriate Bureau Chief.
  • B.Department members authorized to wear the summer detail uniform will:
    • 1.adhere to all other Department uniform and grooming standards.
    • 2.not alter the basic fit or length of the cargo shorts and the shirt sleeves.
  • C.The summer detail uniform is separate from and should not be confused with the approved bicycle uniform.
III.Summer Detail Uniform
  • A.Clothing
    • 1.Cap, baseball-type
    • 2.Footwear, black athletic shoes
    • 3.Overshirt vest carrier (optional)
    • 4.Shirt, short sleeve (police officer - blue, sergeants and above - white)
    • 5.Socks, black or white
    • 6.Shorts, approved cargo
  • B.Uniform Accessories
    • 1.Ammunition pouch and/or magazine case/speedloader
    • 2.Baton holder
    • 3.Belt, trouser
    • 4.Belt, equipment
    • 5.Belt keepers, 4 minimum (members hired after 14 February 2012)
    • 6.Handcuff case (when handcuffs are carried)
    • 7.Holster
    • 8.Insignia - star, field training officer patch insignia (field training officers only), chevrons (sergeants only), appropriate rank insignia (lieutenants and above)
    • 9.Nameplate or name patch
    • 10.Patches, CPD and City Flag shoulder patches
    • 11.Personal aerosol OC chemical device holder
    • 12.Portable radio transceiver attachment
    • 13.Unit assignment designator
    • 14.Traffic Safety Vest
  • C.Equipment
    • 1.Ammunition, Department issued/approved
    • 2.Ball point pen (black ink)
    • 3.Baton, Department approved
    • 4.Body Armor, Department issued/approved
    • 5.Handcuffs (members hired after 1 December 1991)
    • 6.Helmet, Department approved
    • 7.Authorized Firearm
    • 8.Prescribed flashlight
    • 9.Personal aerosol OC chemical device
  • D.Miscellaneous
    • 1.Department Photo Identification Card, Driver's License, and Firearm Owner's Identification (FOID) card
    • 2.Watch or other timekeeping device
    • 3.Whistle
