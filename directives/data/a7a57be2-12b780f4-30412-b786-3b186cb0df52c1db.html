Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S04-26-02
Elevator, Escalator and Amusement Ride Accidents
Issue Date:07 March 1992Effective Date:08 March 1992
Rescinds:S92-01
Index Category:Preliminary Investigations
I.Purpose
This directive continues procedures to be followed when accidents involving elevators, escalators or amusement rides are brought to the attention of the Department.
II.Reporting Procedures
  • A.A member investigating an accident involving an elevator, escalator or amusement ride will:
    • 1.prepare the appropriate case report, indicating that an extra copy be forwarded to the  Bureau of Elevator Inspection, Department of Buildings
