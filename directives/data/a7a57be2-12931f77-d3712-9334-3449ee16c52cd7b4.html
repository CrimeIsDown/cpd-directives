Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S05-06
Active Shooter Incident Plan
Issue Date:15 August 2008Effective Date:16 August 2008
Rescinds:G08-02
Index Category:Extraordinary Responses
I.Purpose
This directive designates the responsibility for providing appropriate training and evaluation, as needed. Department members will refer to the General Order titled "Active Shooter Incident Plan" for initial procedures and responsibilities when an Active Shooter Incident Plan (ASIP) is initiated.
II.Post-Incident Evaluation
  • A.The district commander of the district of occurrence will:
    • 1.convene a debriefing session with the appropriate area deputy chief and other involved members the day following the incident and forward a summary report to the Assistant Deputy Superintendent, Education and Training Division.
    • 2.ensure that copies of all Bureau of Patrol reports pertaining to the incident are forwarded to the Assistant Deputy Superintendent, Education and Training Division, within ten days after the incident.
  • B.The appropriate area Detective Division and Bomb and Arson Section commanders will ensure that a copy of all reports pertaining to the incident are forwarded to the Assistant Deputy Superintendent, Education and Training Division, when completed.
  • C.Upon receipt of a Recorded Voice Transmission Request, OEMC will forward the following information to the Assistant Deputy Superintendent, Education and Training Division, within ten days after the incident:
    • 1.The event history of the entire incident.
    • 2.The audio recording of the entire incident.
  • D.The Assistant Deputy Superintendent, Education and Training Division, will evaluate the incident and determine the need for supplemental training and dissemination of reports and materials to the appropriate command-level staff.
