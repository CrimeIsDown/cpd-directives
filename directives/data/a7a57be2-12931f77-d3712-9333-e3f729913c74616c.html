Chicago Police DepartmentGeneral Order G05-06
Active Threat Incident Plan
Issue Date:30 July 2019Effective Date:30 July 2019
Rescinds:10 November 2015 Version; S05-06
Index Category:Extraordinary Responses
I.Purpose
This directive delineates the policy and procedures related to the Active Threat Incident Plan and are only accessible to users within the Chicago Police Department network.
II.Active Threat Incident Plan
  • A.This directive is confidential and is for official use only. Department members are prohibited from disseminating, releasing, altering, defacing, or removing any Department record(s) or information unless such action is required as part of their official duties.
  • B.Department members will select "Active Threat Incident Plan" to access the policy and procedures for response to an active threat incident.
