Chicago Police DepartmentUniform and Property U04-01-05
Alternate / Optional Uniform and Equipment Items
Issue Date:24 November 1998Effective Date:25 November 1998
Rescinds:G98-10-05
Index Category:Uniform and Personal Equipment
I.Purpose
This directive sets forth the alternate and optional uniform / equipment items and the specification number which references the item in the Department's Uniform and Equipment Specifications.
II.General Information
  • A.The provisions of this order do not apply to the alternate prescribed and auxiliary firearms as outlined in the Department directive entitled "Department Approved Weapons and Ammunition."
  • B.Alternate and optional uniform / equipment items are authorized for field uniform wear, unless also specified for dress uniform wear.
  • C.The seasonal requirements for alternate and optional uniform / equipment items are listed in this directive.
  • D.Winter uniform garments and equipment will be worn/used from 1 November through 30 April.
  • E.Spring/fall uniform garments and equipment will be worn/used from 1 May through 31 May and 1 October through 31 October.
  • F.Summer uniform garments and equipment will be worn/used from 1 June through 30 September.
  • G.On duty members, unless otherwise specified, will use only those uniform / equipment items listed in the Departments Uniform and Specifications or this directive.
