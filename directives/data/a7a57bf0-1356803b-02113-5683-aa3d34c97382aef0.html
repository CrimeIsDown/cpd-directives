Chicago Police DepartmentUniform and Property U06-06
No Longer Approved for Purchase
Issue Date:18 December 2017Effective Date:18 December 2017
Rescinds:02 May 2017 Version
Index Category:Uniform and Equipment Specifications
I.Purpose
  • A.The uniform items described in the specifications listed below are no longer approved for purchase as of the date listed beside them. When a rescinded specification has been replaced by another specification, the most current uniform specification is also listed (with a hyperlink).
  • B.Members who currently own/possess any item described in the specifications listed in this directive may continue to wear that item until it is no longer serviceable.
  • C.Items described in the specifications listed in this directive that are no longer serviceable will be replaced by an item that meets the most current uniform specification, if applicable.
