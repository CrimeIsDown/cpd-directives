Alternate Uniform Items and Optional Summer Detail Uniform
Issue Date:29 June 2006Effective Date:29 June 2006
Rescinds:D06-26
Index Category:Uniform and Personal Equipment
I.Purpose
This notice:
  • A.continues use of the following alternate uniform items:
    • 1.“Coolmax” summer shirt;
    • 2.Synthetic leather duty gear; and
    • 3.Riot gear.
  • B.continues use of the following uniform configurations:
    • 1.Bomb Technician Uniform, Bomb and Arson Unit; and
    • 2.Optional Summer Detail Uniform.
  • C.provides authorization and guidelines for wearing the summer detail uniform.
II.General Information
The uniform items listed in Item III of this directive have been evaluated, field tested, and meet or exceed the criteria established by the Uniform and Personal Equipment Policy Committee.

IV.Description of Uniform Items
  • A.Synthetic Duty Gear
    The approved synthetic duty gear is lightweight, weather-resistant, abrasion-resistant, maintains color retention, and can be cleaned with soap and water or, in the case of bio-safety concerns, with bleach or a bleach solution.
  • B.Riot Gear
    This gear consists of hard shell impact resistance outerwear and is an alternate to the riot gear prescribed in Uniform Specification No. 9191.220.
  • C.Coolmax shirt
    The Coolmax shirt consists of a highly breathable fabric that channels perspiration to its surface, keeping the wearer drier and more comfortable than traditional fabrics.
  • D.Cargo Shorts
    The eight-pocket cotton/polyester blend cargo shorts consists of a lightweight breathable cotton-base fabric that retains the strength and color of polyester.
V.Description of Additional Uniform Configurations
  • A.Bomb Technician Uniform, Bomb and Arson Section (Specification No. 9164.040)
    The Bomb Technician Uniform is made of 100 percent cotton to reduce friction while performing explosive-related duties. The uniform consists of:
    • 1.100 percent cotton polo shirt (tan);
    • 2.100 percent cotton utility trousers (black);
    • 3.100 percent cotton tactical vest (black);
    • 4.100 percent cotton socks;
    • 5.100 percent cotton waterproof insulated coat (black); and
    • 6.any approved footwear except Corfram/Clarino types.
  • B.Summer Detail Uniform
    • 1.The summer detail uniform consists of the following:
      • a.approved poly/cotton, poly/rayon, or Coolmax short-sleeved shirt;
      • b.white short-sleeved undershirt;
      • c.approved alternate trousers or approved cargo-shorts;
      • d.approved baseball cap;
      • e.matching plain black or white crew-length athletic socks if wearing the shorts;
      • f.approved black athletic shoes;
      • g.approved body armor and ballistic carrier; and
      • h.approved duty gear.
      NOTE:
      The summer detail uniform should not be confused with the bicycle uniform.
    • 2.Department members authorized to wear the summer detail uniform will:
      • a.adhere to regular Department uniform standards.
      • b.NOT alter the basic fit or length of the cargo shorts and the shirt sleeves.
    • 3.Members are authorized to wear the optional summer detail uniform:
      • a.from 01 June through 30 September or when the temperature is expected to exceed 80 degrees; and
      • b.when assigned or detailed to marathons, outdoor concerts, musical festivals, parades, outdoor neighborhood events and festivals, outdoor sporting events, lakefront events, or
      • c.any other event or situation approved in writing by the unit commanding officer, or
      • d.be a member of the Bureau of Patrol.
