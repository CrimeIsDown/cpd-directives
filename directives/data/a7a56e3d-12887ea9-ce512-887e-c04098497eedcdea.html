Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E02-02
Payroll and Timekeeping
Issue Date:27 September 1994Effective Date:29 September 1994
Rescinds:G94-05
Index Category:Payroll and Timekeeping
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.establishes Department payroll and timekeeping policy and procedures for sworn and civilian members. Payroll and timekeeping policy and procedure for prevailing rate employees will be in accordance with existing labor agreements and related Department directives.
  • B.introduces the implementation of the Office Automation Overtime and Out of Grade Computer System.
II.Policy
  • A.The command staff member of the Finance Division:
    • 1.has the authority to interpret all Department payroll and timekeeping policy and procedures.
    • 2.is responsible for implementation of all payroll and timekeeping policy and procedures.
    • 3.is responsible for the preparation and updating of the Timekeeper's Manual.
    • 4.will provide training of Department personnel in timekeeping procedures.
  • B.All policy affecting command staff personnel originates from the Superintendent of Police.
Matt L. Rodriguez
Superintendent of Police
