Chicago Police DepartmentUniform and Property U04-01
Uniforms / Citizen's Dress and Personal Equipment
Issue Date:24 November 1998Effective Date:25 November 1998
Rescinds:G98-10
Index Category:Uniform and Personal Equipment
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.prescribes uniforms and authorized equipment items for sworn and civilian members.
  • B.outlines on-duty citizen's dress requirements for sworn and civilian members.
  • C.continues the Uniform and Personal Equipment Policy Committee.
II.General Information
  • A.The provisions of this directive will ensure uniformity in all items used by Department personnel to enhance appearance and present a professional image.
  • B.The contents of this directive and the Uniform and Equipment Specifications Manual are the uniform / citizen's dress and personal equipment standards of the Chicago Police Department.
Terry G. Hillard
Superintendent of Police
