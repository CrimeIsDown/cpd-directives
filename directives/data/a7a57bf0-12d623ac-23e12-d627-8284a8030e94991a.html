Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E02-01
Work Day Duty Schedules
Issue Date:03 January 2011Effective Date:06 January 2011
Rescinds:S11-01
Index Category:Payroll and Timekeeping
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.informs Department members of the work day duty schedules.
  • B.announces basic payroll guidelines for those assigned to various work day duty schedules.
II.General Information
  • A.The Department utilizes three primary sworn work day duty schedules:
    • 1.8.5 hour tours of duty: 8.0 hours plus 30 minutes for uncompensated lunch periods.
    • 2.9.0 hour tours of duty: 8.5 hours plus 30 minutes for uncompensated lunch periods.
    • 3.10.5 hour tours of duty: 10 hours plus 30 minutes for uncompensated lunch periods.
  • B.Civilian members work an 8 hour tour of duty; 7 hours plus 60 minutes for uncompensated lunch periods.
  • C.The Department utilizes both rotating day off groups and fixed day off groups; the following series of numbers are used to assign members to rotating day off cycles: 1-7, the sixty series (61-66), and the seventy series (71-77).
  • D.All sworn members receive a one half hour uncompensated lunch period. If the member is required to perform work during this one half hour lunch period, the member may request overtime compensation.
  • E.The collective bargaining agreements, under the "Change of Schedule" section, allow the employer the right to adjust the starting time plus or minus two hours from the designated starting times (Exception: Captains, 3 hours).
  • F.Hours in excess of the scheduled workday will be compensated at the overtime rate as outlined in the collective bargaining agreements with the Department.
  • G.A personal day, if used, will be worth a tour of duty. All unused personal days will be worth 8 hours if sold back.
  • H.Members working the 9.0 or 10.5 hour shifts will receive either 8.5 or 10 hours, respectively, of holiday compensation for a holiday occurring on their day off. Personnel working on a designated holiday will receive 8.5 or 10 hours of compensation and 4.25 or 5 hours of holiday compensation with the option of pay or compensatory time.
  • I.Baby furlough days for FOP members are worth 8 hours. Officers working the 9.0 or 10.5 hour shifts electing to use a baby furlough day will be required to supplement the baby furlough day with an additional one-half or two (2) hours of compensatory time, respectively, to account for a tour of duty. Any unused baby furlough days are worth 8 hours if sold back.
  • J.Baby furlough days for sergeants, lieutenants and captains will be considered a tour of duty. Unused baby furlough days will be worth 8 hours if sold back.
III.8.5 Hour Work Day Duty Schedule
All sworn Department members will be assigned to the 8.5 hours schedule unless their unit is authorized by this directive, or the written approval of their unit's respective assistant superintendent, to work an alternative work duty schedule. This duty schedule includes the 30 minute uncompensated lunch period.
IV.9.0 Hour Work Day Duty Schedule
  • A.All Bureau of Patrol districts
    • 1.The normal tour of duty for this work shift will be 9.0 hours with one half hour uncompensated lunch period.
    • 2.Day-off groups will be posted prior to the beginning of each police period. Day off groups are identified on the Department Operations Calendar.
    • 3.The 9.0 hours work schedule WILL NOT apply to the following personnel, who will continue to work the 8.5 hour schedule:
      • a.Personnel in fixed day off groups, i.e., Sat / Sun, Fri / Sat, Sun / Mon; and
      • b.Civilians.
  • B.Detective Areas: 1, 3, 4 and 5 - Units 610, 630, 640 and 650
    • 1.The normal tour of duty for the 9.0 hour work shift will be 8.5 hours plus one half hour uncompensated lunch period.
    • 2.Day off groups will be posted prior to the beginning of each police period. The day off groups are identified on the Department Operations Calendar.
    • 3.The 9.0 hour work schedule WILL NOT apply to the following personnel, who will continue to work the 8.5 hour schedule:
      • a.Personnel in fixed day-off groups, i.e., Sat / Sun, Fri / Sat, Sun / Mon; and
      • b.Civilians.
V.10.5 Hour Work Day Duty Schedule
  • A.The normal tour of duty for the 10.5 hour work shift is 10.0 hours plus one half hour uncompensated lunch period.
  • B.Personnel may be assigned to either rotating days off or consecutive fixed days off including a Saturday or Sunday.
    NOTE:
    Members in rotating day off groups will be assigned to the "70" series: 71-77. For the 1st police period, 2011 those in DOG 1 will be assigned to DOG 71; 2 to 72, - 7 to 77.
  • C.The 10.5 hour work schedule WILL NOT apply to civilians.
VI.Authorization for Alternative Work Day Schedules
  • A.Command staff members requesting that their unit, or specific sub-groups of personnel within their command, work alternative work day schedules will make the request through their chain of command to their respective assistant superintendent.
  • B.The Management and Labor Affairs Section maintains the list of the authorized units, as well as a descriptions of those functions of select personnel within certain units, which are approved by an assistant superintendent to work alternative work hours.
VII.Furloughs
Personnel with:
  • A.twenty or twenty-four straight day furloughs will be given the same number of straight furlough days.
  • B.twenty-two or twenty-five working day furloughs will have those days converted to hours.
  • C.twenty-two working days will receive 176 hours of furlough (members must supplement the last day with the appropriate amount of compensatory hours).
  • D.twenty-five working days will receive 200 hours of furlough (members must supplement the last day with the appropriate amount of compensatory hours).
