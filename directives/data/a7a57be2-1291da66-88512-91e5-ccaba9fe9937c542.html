Chicago Police DepartmentGeneral Order G01-02-01
Organization and Functions of the Office of the Superintendent
Issue Date:29 December 2010Effective Date:29 December 2010
Rescinds:General Order 09-02-1A
Index Category:Department Organization
I.Purpose
This directive establishes the organization and functions of the Office of the Superintendent.
II.Organizational and Functional Units
  • A.Chief of Staff
    The Chief of Staff to the Superintendent performs tasks at the direction of the Superintendent to recommend and coordinate the timely implementation of new policy.
  • B.General Counsel to the Superintendent
    The General Counsel provides advice about legal and legislative matters and various labor agreements as they relate to the administration of the Department. The General Counsel also serves as liaison between the Department, legislative bodies, and other agencies.
  • C.Department Administration
    Department Administration is commanded by a commander who reports directly to the Superintendent. Department Administration coordinates activities and initiatives at the direction of the Superintendent.
  • D.Office of News Affairs
    The Office of News Affairs is commanded by a director who reports directly to the Superintendent. This office serves as liaison between the Department and the news media.
Jody P. Weis
Superintendent of Police
