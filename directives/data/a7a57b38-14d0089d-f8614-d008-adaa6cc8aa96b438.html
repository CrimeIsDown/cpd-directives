I.PORTABLE RADIO HOLDER
  • A.DESIGN:
    The portable radio holder will be specifically designed for and allow proper fitment of a Department-issued radio.
  • B.MATERIALS:
    • 1.Portable radio holders may be constructed of leather, leather-like synthetic materials, woven nylon polymer, or a rigid composite material including, but not limited to, Kydex, Zytel or carbon-fiber depending upon style of duty belt.
    • 2.All hardware will be white or black metal.
  • C.COLOR:
    Black
  • D.FINISH:
    The finish of the portable radio holder will be black in color, free of ornamentation, wrinkles, or defects, and will be smooth without basket weave or high-gloss finish.
