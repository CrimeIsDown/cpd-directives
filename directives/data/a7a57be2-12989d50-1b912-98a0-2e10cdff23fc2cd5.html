Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E05-06
Appointments to D-2A and D-2 Positions
Issue Date:21 February 2012Effective Date:21 February 2012
Rescinds:Version dated 16 May 2003; S03-07
Index Category:Career Development
I.Purpose
This directive informs sworn members below the rank of sergeant that eligibility requirements and selection procedures for D-2A and D-2 positions will be announced at the time applications are being accepted for those particular positions.
II.D-2A Detective Positions
  • A.As the need exists, a Department directive will be issued indicating full application procedures for appointment to the Bureau of Detectives.
  • B.The Superintendent of Police will have final approval on all D-2A appointments.
III.D-2 Positions
  • A.As the need exists, a Department directive will be issued indicating full application procedures for D-2 positions. The following is a list of D-2 appointments:
    • 1.Armorer
    • 2.Canine Handler
    • 3.Explosive Detection Canine Handler
    • 4.Evidence Technician
    • 5.Field Training Officer
    • 6.Fingerprint Examiner
    • 7.Marine Unit Officer
    • 8.Mounted Unit Officer
    • 9.Police Agent
    • 10.Police Technician
    • 11.Traffic Specialist
  • B.The Superintendent of Police will have final approval on all D-2 appointments.
IV.Eligibility / Selection Procedure
  • A.The eligibility requirements and the selection procedures that will be used in determining successful applicants to be appointed to D-2A and D-2 positions will be announced by the Department at the time that the Department directive indicating the application process is published.
  • B.Applicants must be sworn members below the rank of sergeant who are physically able to perform the tasks required at the time of appointment.
  • C.Some appointments may require an applicant to be placed on an eligibility list and selected as vacancies become available.
  • D.The Department reserves the right to fill 20% of the recognized vacancies for all D-2A and D-2 positions at its discretion without regard to seniority or rank score, provided that the position is filled by a candidate on the respective eligibility list.
Items indicated by italic/double underline were added or revised.
