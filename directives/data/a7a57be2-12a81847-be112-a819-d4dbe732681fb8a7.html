Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S09-03-02
Forms Management System
Issue Date:06 May 2009Effective Date:06 May 2009
Rescinds:A09-03
Index Category:Information Management
I.Purpose
This directive outlines responsibilities and procedures for the administration of the Chicago Police Department’s forms management system.
II.Responsibilities
The Research and Development Division has the responsibility for:
  • A.creating new CPD forms from a prototype.
    NOTE:
    It is the requester’s responsibility for developing the concept of a new form and supplying a draft to the Research and Development Division.
  • B.revising existing CPD forms.
  • C.discontinuing CPD forms.
  • D.automating (read / write PDF format) CPD forms.
  • E.managing the CPD forms numbering system.
  • F.coordinating the printing of CPD forms.
    NOTE:
    The Reproduction and Graphic Arts Section will not honor requests for duplication of forms unless the Reproduction & Graphic Arts Service Requisition (CPD-34.705) has been approved by the Research and Development Division.
  • G.maintaining CPD form files which include history, printing specifications, copies of print requests, annual usage, samples, and any other related information.
  • H.requesting retention periods for new CPD forms through the Local Records Commission and the Illinois State Archives.
  • I.maintaining the CPD Forms Retention Schedule (CPD-11.717) and the CPD Department Record Index consistent with the Department directive entitled ”Records Management.”
III.Procedures
  • A.Unit commanding officers requesting a new or revised CPD form will attach a draft copy with a Request for New CPD Form (CPD-15.272) or a Request for Revised CPD Form (CPD-15.273) to the Research and Development Division.
  • B.Unit commanding officers may request that an existing CPD form be made available on the Department’s intranet by sending the Request to Automate an Existing CPD Form (CPD-15.274) directly to the Research and Development Division. Not all forms, such as multiple-part forms, can be automated.
  • C.Unit commanding officers requesting to discontinue CPD forms originated / owned by their unit will submit the Request to Discontinue CPD Form (CPD-15.271) directly to the Research and Development Division.
  • D.If a CPD form is stored at the Equipment and Supply Section, form supply requests will be made using the Material Requisition (CPD-34.622) form submitted directly to the Equipment and Supply Section. Requests to reprint CPD forms stored exclusively at the requesting unit will be made by contacting the Research and Development Division.
  • E.Information requests relating to the creation, revision, automation, retention, printing, storage, and history of CPD forms will be directed to the Research and Development Division. For more specific procedures and information relative to CPD forms, visit the Research and Development Division webpage on the Department’s intranet listed under “Department Forms Information.”
