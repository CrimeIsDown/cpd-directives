Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E02-03-04
Time and Attendance Record – Detailed Personnel
Issue Date:13 April 2001Effective Date:15 April 2001
Rescinds:D01-19-04
Index Category:Payroll and Timekeeping
I.Purpose
This directive enumerates specific procedures for the preparation of the Time and Attendance Record for members detailed to another unit.
II.Procedures
  • A.When a member is “detailed out,” the timekeeper at the unit of assignment will send the member’s primary Time and Attendance Record to the unit of detail and keep a copy of the Time and Attendance Record in the unit of assignment.
    NOTE:
    The Time and Attendance Record will not be sent when a member is detailed to an area mission team. During the area mission detail, daily entries will be made on the member’s primary Time and Attendance Record by the timekeeper in the detailed member’s unit of assignment.
  • B.The member’s primary Time and Attendance Record will be annotated to reflect the date of detail and will be retained in the unit of detail files. This annotation will be recorded by pencil entry on the top of the card above the heading entitled “Social Security No.” When the members returns to the unit of assignment, the date of return will be entered next to the date of detail.
  • C.The member’s photocopied Time and Attendance Record will also be annotated to reflect the date of detail and will be retained in the unit of assignment files. This annotation will be recorded in ink on the top of the card above the heading entitled “Social Security No.”
  • D.At the completion of the detail, the primary Time and Attendance Record will be returned to the unit of assignment and filed with the photocopied Time and Attendance Record in the unit of assignment files.
  • E.The timekeeper of the unit of detail will, IN ADDITION TO recording the appropriate entries on the primary Time and Attendance Record, notify the timekeeper of the unit of assignment if the pay or non-pay status of the detailed member must be recorded on the City of Chicago Time Roll (CO 539) as indicted in the Department directive entitled “Time Roll Procedures.” The timekeeper of the unit of assignment will then make the appropriate entry on the Time Roll.
