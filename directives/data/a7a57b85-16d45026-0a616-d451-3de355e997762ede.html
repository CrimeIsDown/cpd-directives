I.CHICAGO POLICE MEMORIAL PIN
  • A.DESIGN:
    Multi-colored 2 inch wide by ½ inch high metal bar pin with dual clasp back worn to recognize the Chicago Police Memorial Foundation. The Chicago Police Department Foundation Memorial pin is available for sworn and civilian members. The pin must be worn centered on the shirt left pocket flap ½ inch below the finished edge. On shirts without pocket flaps the pin will be worn ½ inch above the center of the left pocket. Outer garments with a flapped breast pocket will wear the pin centered ½ inch below the left side finished flap edge. Outer garments without a left side flapped pocket will wear the pin positioned centered ½ inch above the pocket edge.
  • B.MATERIAL:
    Cloisenne metal
  • C.COLOR:
    • 1.Medium blue top portion
    • 2.Black and white center checkerboard
    • 3.Dark blue bottom.
    • 4.White lettering with silver Chicago Police Department Star
    • 5.Silver border.
  • D.CONSTRUCTION:
    • 1.Border will be 1/32 inch wide and nickle plated
    • 2.The top portion will be medium blue.
    • 3.The words “CHICAGO POLICE” will be 1/8 inch high in white letters.
    • 4.The Chicago Police star will be centered between the two words.
    • 5.The checkerboard will be black and white alternating 3/16 inch high.
    • 6.The bottom portion will be dark blue with white lettering 1/8 inch high which spell “MEMORIAL FOUNDATION”
    • 7.The back of the bar will be nickel plated with a dual clutch-back fastener.
