Watch
Issue Date:26 August 2019Effective Date:26 August 2019
Rescinds:U23.0100
Index Category:Uniform and Equipment Specifications
I.WATCH
  • A.DESIGN:
    Standard sized wristwatch or pocketwatch without chain or fob utilizing a dial or digital display covered with a clear display covering.
  • B.MATERIAL:
    • 1.Metal case
    • 2.Plastic case
    • 3.Cloth, plastic, rubber, or metal band
    • 4.Blend of the above materials
  • C.COLOR:
    Assorted, non-iridescent, non-bright
  • D.CONSTRUCTION:
    • 1.Dial: Maximum 2 1/2 inches diameter
    • 2.Strap: Maximum 2 1/2 inches width
