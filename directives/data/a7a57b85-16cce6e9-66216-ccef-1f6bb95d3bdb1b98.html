Chicago Police DepartmentUniform and Property U06-01-31
Helmet - General Duty, Vehicular, and Ballistic
Issue Date:04 November 2020Effective Date:04 November 2020
Rescinds:26 August 2019 Version
Index Category:Uniform and Equipment Specifications
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.provides specifications for General Duty, Vehicular, and Ballistic Helmets.
  • B.satisfies CALEA Law Enforcement Standard Chapter 41.
II.Policy
All sworn personnel are required to own and maintain the appropriate prescribed helmets listed in this directive.
III.General Information
The prescribed Department helmet will be multi-functional for general/riot duty and vehicular use designed to work with all body armor and gas masks.
IV.Approved Department Helmet - Specifications
  • A.General Duty Helmet
    • 1.Design:
      • a.The helmet will provide a combination of safety and comfort designed for emergency response teams and other tactical uses requiring a special-purpose helmet.
      • b.The helmet must conform to the head and provide certified protection under NIJ STD 0104.02 and FMVSS 218 (D.O.T.).
      • c.The helmet will have a transparent black snap-on visor, cushion-padded interior, comfort throat sleeve, rear nape strap and sleeve, quick-release strap, foul-weather adapter, and tactical duty guard.
      • d.Face-shield cover constructed of synthetic leather.
      • e.Decals and star number will be properly affixed following the contour points of the star emblem.
      • f.Ventilated suspension system for cool and comfortable adjustments to all head sizes.
    • 2.Materials:
      • a.Fiberglass.
      • b.Polycarbon.
    • 3.Color:
      • a.Chicago Police Department Light Blue.
      • b.High-gloss clear coat with black trim.
    • 4.Construction:
      • a.Sizes: extra-small, small, medium, large, and extra-large.
      • b.Weight: 32-36 ounces
        General Duty-S1602W-CPD

  • C.Ballistic Helmet
    • 1.Design:
      • a.The helmet will provide a combination of safety and comfort designed for emergency response teams and other tactical uses requiring a special-purpose helmet.
      • b.The helmet must conform to the head and provide certified protection for the energy absorbing aramid outer shell and will deliver an impact system that exceeds the requirements of NIJ 0106.01 Level IIIA ballistic helmets.
      • c.The helmet will have a transparent black snap-on visor, cushion-padded interior, comfort throat sleeve, rear nape strap and sleeve, quick-release strap, foul-weather adapter, and tactical duty guard.
      • d.Laser beam face-shield cover constructed of synthetic leather.
      • e.Decals and star number will be properly affixed following the contour points of the star emblem.
      • f.Ventilated suspension system for cool and comfortable adjustments to all head sizes.
    • 2.Materials:
      • a.Multi-layered aramid composite laminate under heat and pressure meeting the NIJ 0106.01 Level IIIA ballistic requirement. Outer shell must be a smooth non-textured surface made of aramid fibers for increased strength-to-weight ratio. For additional coverage, the shell will have a wide cut around the ears to reduce interference from other equipment.
      • b.Shell edge will be furnished with a tough molded scuff-proof edging. Shell finish will be painted Chicago Police Department Light Blue. Polane, scratch-resistant, chip-resistant paint must be used to increase durability of the painted surface.
      • c.A vinyl CPD star decal will be applied to the painted surface, under the high-gloss clear coat. A top coat of automotive quality urethane high-gloss UV protectant clear coat must be applied to protect the paint and CPD star decal.
      • d.Ear/Neck protector is made from .60” thick ABS with 3/8” high-density closed-cell foam designed for high-impact shock protection against projectiles.
      • e.Face shield is .175” thick high-impact optically superior polycarbonate designed to fit over the Avon 50 gas masks and FM 12 respirators.
      • f.Face shield is equipped with a neoprene rubber seal to prevent liquids from entering into the face area and eyes.
      • g.Face shield will be equipped with a laser beam protective strip applied to the inside in order to protect the laser beam strip from chemicals and projectiles.
      • h.The laser beam protective strip will have the capability of a one watt green or blue laser beam attack from a distance of two yards, for a stationary of ten seconds, and reduce the eye exposure to 1.0 watt.
      • i.The laser beam protective strip has an optical density of OD 3.0 for 520-532nm wavelengths for a period of ten seconds.
        NOTE:
        The OD scale is based on log 10 reduction. Therefore, the OD1.0 is 10% reduction, OD2.0 is 100%, OD3.0 is 1000% reduction.
      • j.The laser beam protective strip will allow the officer to have a clear normal vision by depressing the head to engage the laser protection when needed.
    • 3.Color:
      • a.Chicago Police Department Light Blue.
      • b.High-gloss clear coat with black trim.
    • 4.Construction:
      • a.Sizes: extra-small, small, medium, large, extra-large, 2X Large, 3XLarge.
      • b.Weight 2.9 lbs.-3.2 lbs.
        S1711-CPD Ballistic Helmet
V.Authorized Accessories
  • A.Face shield, full flip, shatterproof UV-treated polycarbon with screw attachment and liquid seal. Face shield will meet or exceed NIJ Standard-0104.02 for riot protection and will be designed to fit over both the Avon C50 and FM12 respirators.
  • B.Face shield cover constructed of synthetic leather.
  • C.Helmet carry bag constructed of Cordura with double zipper and handles.
  • D.Ear and neck protector.
VI.AUTHORIZATIONS AND RESTRICTIONS
  • A.If a Department member damages or loses the helmet, the Chief, Office of Operations, must
    be informed via a To-From-Subject-Report submitted through the chain of command.
  • B.Recruits must purchase their duty helmet through the Training Division.
  • C.Decals and star numbers will be Department issued.
  • D.Duty helmets will be worn by members of the Motorcycle Unit.
  • E.Duty helmets will be worn by members of the Mounted Unit when conditions/situations deem it necessary or with prior notification.
  • F.The Duty helmet will only be worn by members when conditions/situations deem it necessary or with prior notification.
