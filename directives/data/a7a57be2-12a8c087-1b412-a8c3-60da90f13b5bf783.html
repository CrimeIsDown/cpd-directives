Court Branch Assignment Schedule
Issue Date:05 June 2015Effective Date:05 June 2015
Rescinds:1 November 2014 Version
Index Category:Processing Persons
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.designates the branch courts and court calls Department members will use to schedule court cases.
  • B.outlines a schedule for prioritizing cases for assignment to the appropriate branch court by district.
    NOTE:
    Arrests effected by members of units not assigned a court call will be scheduled for the appropriate court based on the type of offense and district in which the offense occurred.
