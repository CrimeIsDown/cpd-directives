Cap Shield
Issue Date:18 September 2019Effective Date:18 September 2019
Rescinds:U9.1419
Index Category:Uniform and Equipment Specifications
I.CAP SHIELD
  • A.DESIGN:
    A plated, polished, and properly finished identification designator, featuring the Seal of the City of Chicago, a Department identifier, and a rank/position identifier for sergeants.
  • B.MATERIALS:
    Metal
  • C.COLOR:
    • 1.Police Officers: Silver metal
    • 2.Sergeants and Above: Gold metal.
  • D.CONSTRUCTION:
    • 1.Dimensions: Cap shield will be of size and thickness appropriate for the member’s rank.
    • 2.Attaching Device: The cap shield will be equipped with two integral screw posts (with screw buttons) on its undersurface.
