I.Purpose
This directive delineates the auto pounds to which vehicles will be towed.

  • B.HOLD - CONFIDENTIAL VIN CHECKS
    Vehicles towed in order to have confidential VIN checks performed will be towed to Auto Pound 1.
  • C.HOLD FOR INVESTIGATIONS
    Vehicles towed to be held for investigation or those involved in a death will be towed to Auto Pound 1. As special circumstances dictate, investigating members may have vehicles towed as a "hold for investigation" to another location.
  • D.SEIZURES
    Vehicles towed and held for seizure review for forfeiture will be towed to Auto Pound 1.
  • E.IMPOUNDMENTS
    Impoundment of vehicles for municipal code violations will be towed to Auto Pounds 2 (South) and 6 (North).
  • F.SNOW TOWS
    Snow tows will be towed to Auto Pounds 2, 6, and Central.
  • G.ABANDONED TOWS
    All abandoned vehicle tows city-wide will be towed to Auto Pounds 2 (South) and 6 (North).
