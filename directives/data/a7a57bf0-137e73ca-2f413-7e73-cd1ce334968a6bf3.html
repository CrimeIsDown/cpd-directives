I.PURPOSE
  • A.This addendum:
    • 1.identifies the:
      • a.date, time, and locations of the Summer 2012 “Don’t Kill A Dream, Save A Life” gun turn-in program.
      • b.debit card amount given to the participant for each weapon turned in.
    • 2.delineates the responsibilities of Department members participating in the gun turn-in program for Summer 2012.
  • B.Department members will refer to the Special Order "Gun Turn-In Program" for additional procedural information.

  • B.If a weapon turned in qualifies as an assault weapon, firearm, B-B gun/air gun, or replica gun, as defined in the parent order of this directive, a debit card or a combination of debit cards to total the following denominations will be given to the participant as follows:
    • 1.$100 for each firearm;
    • 2.$10 for each B-B gun, air gun, or replica gun.
III.RESPONSIBILITIES
  • A.The Education and Training Division will provide officers assigned to the Firearms Training Unit to receive the weapons from the participants and ensure they are rendered clear and safe before being processed.
  • B.The Forensic Services Division will provide:
    • 1.officers to hand carry the firearms to the Evidence and Recovered Property Section for completion of the firearm inventories.
    • 2.firearms technicians to receive the weapons from the participants and ensure it is rendered clear and safe before it is processed.
  • C.The Bureau of Organized Crime will provide officers from the CAGE Team to receive the weapons from the participants and ensure they are rendered clear and safe before being processed.
  • D.The Chief, Bureau of Patrol, will designate appropriate personnel to assist in the operations of this program.
