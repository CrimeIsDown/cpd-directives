Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S04-13-05
Alternate Paper Reporting Procedures
Issue Date:07 August 2007Effective Date:07 August 2007
Rescinds:D07-33
Index Category:Preliminary Investigations
I.Purpose
This directive informs Department members of alternate paper reporting procedures utilized for the CLEAR automated arrest system, the eTrack inventory system, Tactical Response Reports, and Officer Battery Reports when the CLEAR system is unavailable and paper reporting has been authorized.
NOTE:
This directive references relevant current directives and is to be used as a general guide to expedite the transition when using alternate paper reporting procedures.
II.CLEAR Automated Arrest System
  • A.Once paper procedures are approved by the On-Call Incident Commander, Department members will use the Arrest Report (CPD- 11.420).
  • B.If members obtained a tracking / Central Booking (CB) number and then were unable to complete the procedure because the CLEAR system became unavailable, members will:
    • 1.complete an Arrest Report; and
    • 2.once the CLEAR system is available, verify the tracking / CB number and fax a To-From-Subject report to the Field Services Section, requesting to delete the CB number obtained before the CLEAR system became unavailable.
  • C.When the CLEAR system becomes available, the lockup keeper will login to the CHRIS client server application and use the CHRIS Manage Booking module to enter required arrestee data from the paper Arrest Report. Once the data entry process is completed and the “Booking Posted” button selected, fingerprints and mug shots can be captured on the appropriate workstations.
    NOTE:
    Lockup keepers will refer to the on-line user’s guide entitled “Paper Arrest Procedures.”
  • D.For complete procedures, members will refer to the Department directive entitled “Clear Automated Arrest System,” and the Department directive entitled “Arrest Report and Related Documents.”
III.eTrack Inventory System
  • A.Once approved by the watch commander or command staff, members will inventory property using the Property Inventory Book (CPD-34.523). The buff copy of the property inventory formset will be sent to the Evidence and Recovered Property Section or the Forensic Services Section, as appropriate, via Department mail.
    NOTE:
    It is the watch commander’s responsibility to ensure an appropriate supply of Property Inventory Books are available in the district.
  • B.For complete procedures, members will refer to the Department directive entitled “Phase 2 eTrack Inventory System for Property Taken into Custody,” and the Department directive entitled “Inventory System for Property Taken into Custody.”
IV.Tactical Response Reports (TRR) and Officer Battery Reports (OBR)
  • A.Once approved by the Public Safety Information Technology (PSIT), members will complete paper TRRs and OBRs and distribute them according to the procedures on the form.
    NOTE:
    It is the watch commander’s responsibility to ensure an appropriate supply of TRRs and OBRs are available in the district.
  • B.For complete procedures, members will refer to the Department directive entitled “CLEAR Automated Tactical Response Report,” and the Department directive entitled “Incidents Requiring the Completion of a Tactical Response Report.”
V.CLEAR System Availability
Units will be notified via AMFN when the automated arrest system, the eTrack inventory system, and the automated TRRs and OBRs resume operation.
