Holster—Firearm
Issue Date:26 August 2019Effective Date:26 August 2019

Index Category:Uniform and Equipment Specifications
I.Purpose
This specification describes holsters for prescribed, alternate, and auxiliary firearms.
II.HOLSTER – UNIFORMED MEMBERS ASSIGNED TO FIELD DUTIES HIRED AFTER 01 JANUARY 2008
  • A.DESIGN
    The holster will be designed so that:
    • 1.when the member is standing, the firearm will be in a vertical or near vertical position.
    • 2.the firearm is not canted in excess of 45 degrees in any direction.
    • 3.The holster will affix to a 2 ¼ inch duty belt.
    • 4.the bottom of the holster does not extend more than 10 inches below the top of the trouser.
    • 5.the holster hangs parallel to the outside seam of the trouser leg.
    • 6.the inside grip of the firearm does not extend more than 3 inches from the horizontal form of the holster’s shank.
    • 7.the muzzle of the firearm does not extend more than ½ inch below the bottom of the holster.
  • B.MATERIALS
    • 1.Holsters may be constructed of leather, leather-like synthetic materials, or a rigid composite material including, but not limited to, Kydex, Zytel, or carbon-fiber.
    • 2.All hardware will be silver colored or black metal.
  • C.COLOR
    All exposed surfaces of the holster will be black in color.
  • D.CONSTRUCTION
    • 1.The holster will be constructed of one or more pieces, properly molded and shaped to fit the firearm, and allow for attachment directly to prescribed duty belts.
    • 2.Rivets, screws, or other fasteners may be used in the construction of the holster.
      NOTE:
      Metal components of the holster will not come into contact with the firearm.
    • 3.Holsters will have a minimum of two manual retention devices, requiring deliberate manipulation by the shooter to defeat the retention device and free the firearm from the holster.
      NOTE:
      Acceptable manual retention device examples include two snaps, a single snap combined with a rocking motion or a rotating hood with sentry-type switch installed and in use.
  • E.FINISH
    The finish of the holster will be black in color, free of wrinkles or defects, and will be smooth without basket weave or high-gloss finish.

V.HOLSTER – PRESCRIBED, MEMBERS HIRED BEFORE 01 JANUARY 2008
  • A.DESIGN
    The holster will be designed so that:
    • 1.when the member is standing, the firearm will be in a vertical or near vertical position.
    • 2.the firearm is not canted in excess of 45 degrees in any direction.
    • 3.the holster will affix to a trouser or duty belt.
    • 4.the bottom of the holster does not extend more than 10 inches below the top of the trouser.
    • 5.the holster hangs parallel to the outside seam of the trouser leg.
    • 6.the inside grip of the firearm does not extend more than 3 inches from the horizontal form of the holster’s shank.
    • 7.the muzzle of the firearm does not extend more than ½ inch below the bottom of the holster.
    • 8.Sight tracks and sight guards are authorized.
  • B.MATERIALS
    • 1.Holsters may be constructed of leather, leather-like synthetic materials, or a rigid composite material including, but not limited to, Kydex, Zytel, or carbon-fiber.
    • 2.All hardware will be white or black metal.
  • C.COLOR
    All exposed surfaces of the holster will be black in color.
  • D.CONSTRUCTION
    • 1.The holster will be constructed of one or more pieces, properly molded and shaped to fit the firearm, and allow for attachment directly to prescribed trouser or duty belts. Swivel and clam shell type holsters are prohibited.
    • 2.Holsters will be tightly stitched with black thread.
    • 3.Rivets, screws, or other fasteners may be used to reinforce stress points.
      NOTE:
      Metal components of the holster will not come into contact with the firearm.
    • 4.Holsters will have a minimum of one manual retention device, requiring deliberate manipulation by the shooter to defeat the retention device and free the firearm from the holster.
      NOTE:
      'Velcro' type retaining straps on exposed holsters are prohibited.
  • E.FINISH
    The finish of the holster will be black in color, free of wrinkles or defects, and will be smooth without basket weave or high-gloss finish.

IX.HOLSTER – AUXILIARY SUBCOMPACT SEMIAUTOMATIC PISTOLS – ANKLE AND POCKET HOLSTERS
  • A.ANKLE HOLSTER
    • 1.DESIGN:
      The holster will be designed so that the muzzle of the firearm does not extend more than ½ inch below the bottom of the holster. Ankle holsters will be designed and worn so that it is completely concealed by the bottom of the trousers.
    • 2.MATERIALS
      Holsters may be constructed of leather, leather-like synthetic materials, or a rigid composite material including, but not limited to, Kydex, Zytel, or carbon-fiber.
    • 3.COLOR
      Assorted
    • 4.FINISH
      Assorted
  • B.POCKET HOLSTER
    • 1.DESIGN:
      The holster will be designed so that the muzzle of the firearm does not extend more than ½ inch below the bottom of the holster. Pocket holsters will be designed and worn so that it is completely concealed within an appropriate garment pocket.
    • 2.MATERIALS
      Holsters may be constructed of leather, leather-like synthetic materials, or a rigid composite material including, but not limited to, Kydex, Zytel, or carbon-fiber.
    • 3.COLOR
      Assorted
    • 4.CONSTRUCTION
      • 1.The holster will be constructed of one or more pieces, properly molded and shaped to fit the firearm.
      • 2.Holsters will cover the trigger and trigger guard.
      • 3.Retaining strap or thumb-break device is optional.
      • 4.Open top holsters will be constructed so as to hold the firearm in place during activity.
    • 5.FINISH
      Assorted
