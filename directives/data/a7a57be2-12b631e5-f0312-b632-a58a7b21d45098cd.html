Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E02-03-02
Sworn Time and Attendance Record – Command Staff Non-Bargaining Unit Members
Issue Date:13 April 2001Effective Date:15 April 2001
Rescinds:D01-19-02
Index Category:Payroll and Timekeeping
I.Purpose
This directive establishes procedure for completing the Time and Attendance Record for non-bargaining unit members.
II.Sworn Supervisor Time and Attendance Record (CPD-11.602-A)
  • A.The portion of the Sworn Supervisor Time and Attendance Record utilized for recording the daily duty status of a non-bargaining unit command staff member will be completed as follows:
    • 1.the unshaded boxes on the top line will be used to record the member's daily duty status. (See examples provided at the end of this directive).
    • 2.the second, third and fourth lines are not utilized.
  • B.The summary columns under the headings “IOD-SP, V, FMLA-AN-ML, P” will be utilized in the same manner as for sworn bargaining unit supervisory members.
    NOTE:
    Balances for P and V days will be carried forward as follows: number of days used (number increases with usage)/number of days authorized. For example: a member is authorized 25 V days and uses one in Cycle 1 - the balance will be carried as 1/25 in the V column. When another V day is used in Cycle 2 - the balance will be carried as 2/25 in the V column.
  • C.The boxes numbered 1 through 12 under the heading “FLSA,Actual Time” are not utilized.
  • D.The boxes numbered 13 through 17 under the heading “Paid Overtime” are not utilized.
  • E.The boxes numbered 1 through 6 under the heading “Compensatory Time Hours” are not utilized.
  • F.Box 7 - Carry forward the balance of compensatory time hours earned prior to assignment as a command staff member.
III.Unit Timekeepers
Unit timekeepers are responsible for ensuring that all information is posted on the Time and Attendance Records in the appropriate spaces provided.
