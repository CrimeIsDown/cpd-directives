Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S09-01
Computerized Information Systems
Issue Date:19 January 2011Effective Date:19 January 2011
Rescinds:
Index Category:Information Management
I.Purpose
The following directives:
  • A.continue the Information Systems Development Group (ISDG).
  • B.set forth Department procedures regarding computerized information systems.
Department members will refer to the General Order titled "Computerized Information Systems" for the policy, responsibilities, and security procedures governing computerized information system.
