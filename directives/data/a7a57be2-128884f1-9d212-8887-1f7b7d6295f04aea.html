Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E02-02-12
Distribution of Paychecks
Issue Date:08 March 2002Effective Date:09 March 2002
Rescinds:S02-04
Index Category:Payroll and Timekeeping
I.Purpose
This directive establishes procedures for completing the Payroll Audit Log and security of payroll checks at all district and unit locations.
II.Procedures / Responsibilities
  • A.The Finance Division will complete a Payroll Audit Log for each unit. This log will list the first and last check numbers and any checks that have been removed from or added to the units payroll.
  • B.The unit / watch commander will:
    • 1.ensure that the payroll checks are picked up as directed by the Finance Division via the Automated Facsimile Message Network (AMFN).
    • 2.before the checks are distributed, assign a person to audit and verify the check numbers against the Payroll Audit Log.
    • 3.sign and date the Payroll Audit Log in the appropriate field and ensure the log is returned the same day through the Police Document Services Section to the Payroll Section.
  • C.The assigned auditor is responsible for the verification of the check numbers against the Payroll Audit Log.
    • 1.If all checks are accounted for on the Payroll Audit Log, the auditor will sign and date the log and present it to the unit / watch commander for signature.
    • 2.If any checks are missing from the sequence and not noted by the Finance Division on the Payroll Audit Log, the auditor will immediately:
      • a.notify the unit / watch commander.
      • b.notify the Payroll Section.
      • c.record on the Unit / District Use Only field of the Payroll Audit Log the missing check number and, if available, the name of the unit member issued the check; sign and date the log; and fax it to the Payroll Section.
        NOTE:
        When completing the log, the auditor will attempt to determine the name of the unit member issued the check; however, notification to the Payroll Section will not be delayed for this reason.
      • d.present the Payroll Audit Log to the watch commander for approval and signature.
  • D.Subsequent unit / watch commanders will ensure that the security of paychecks not yet distributed is maintained.
