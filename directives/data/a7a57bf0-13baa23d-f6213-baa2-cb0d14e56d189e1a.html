III.Revised Maps
A complete set of revised beat, district, area, and radio zone maps are available via CLEAR GIS on the Department intranet. Department members will refer to the updated maps when determining the district/beat of occurrence for an incident reported to the Department and determining the proper radio zone for a specific district or location.
