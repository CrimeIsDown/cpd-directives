Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S02-05-05
Business/Organization Location Record File
Issue Date:02 April 1992Effective Date:03 April 1992
Rescinds:S92-02
Index Category:Human Rights and Community Partnerships
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.continues the use of the Business/Organization Location Record File which enables district personnel to contact a responsible person when an emergency situation occurs during hours when no one is available on the premises.
  • B.outlines the responsibilities for maintenance of the file.
II.Responsibilities
  • A.District commanders will:
    • 1.periodically assign personnel to visit all business establishments in their district. The information obtained during these visits will be recorded on a Business/Organization Location Record card (CPD-21.906). The card will be placed in the Business/Organization Location Record File and will be located in the desk area for police use only.
    • 2.ensure that assignments to update the file do not interfere with the primary police objectives of the assigned personnel.
  • B.Each district will:
    • 1.maintain a supply of Business/Organization Location Record cards to be used to update the file.
    • 2.furnish a card to a business proprietor visiting the district station to provide information.
    • 3.ensure that this file is properly maintained and available to Department members at all times.
