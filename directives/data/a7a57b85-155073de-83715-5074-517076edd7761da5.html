I.RIBBON BARS - CONSTRUCTION
  • A.Material
    Nylon and Dacron composition "campaign" ribbon material
  • B.Metal Frame
    As seen form top view, the frame shall be rectangular, one piece, etched yellow, and measure approximately 1 7/16 inches in length by 7/16 inch high. The frame will have a rectangular "window" which measures 1 1/4 inches by 1/4 inch. The frame shall have four sides at 90 degrees to the frame face and measure approximately 1/16 inch in width. Integrated as part of the one-piece frame with formed sides are four fold tabs equally spaced on the long side of the frame (two per side). These tabs when folded shall fasten the frame to the u-channel ribbon assembly.
  • C.Ribbon Assembly
    Typical u-channel component with ribbon fastened to and wrapped around the channel. Top view assembly dimensions are to be approximately 1 3/8 inches in length and 3/8 inch wide.
  • D.Fastener Assembly
    Typical pin and clutch back fastener on a bar which slips into a u-channel component.

II.RIBBON BARS - ATTACHMENTS
  • A.Stars
    • 1.Design: The star attachment will be a five-pointed star with sharp ridges and points. The star will measure approximately 3/16 inch in diameter and will have two pliable piercing pins, each measuring approximately ¼ inch long.
    • 2.Material: Metal, various alloys.
    • 3.The Department authorizes the use of award attachment stars in three colors, unless otherwise stated in a Department directive. Colors:
      • a.Dark blue enamel- to be worn on ribbon bars for one
        through four receptions of the same award.
      • b.Silver- to be worn on ribbon bars for the fifth
        reception of the same award in lieu of the four blue
        enameled stars.
      • c.Gold- to be worn on ribbon bars for the fifteenth
        reception of the same award in lieu of the silver and
        blue enameled stars.
  • B.Oak Leaves
    • 1.Design: The oak leaf attachment will be a two-dimensional, custom-cast design. The oak leaf will measure approximately 5/16 inch in length by 1/8 inch in width and will have two pliable piercing pins, each measuring approximately ¼ inch long.
    • 2.Material: Metal, various alloys.
    • 3.Colors:
      • a.Antique cast bronze to be worn on Honorable Mention Award bar for 90-149 Honorable Mentions.
      • b.Bright silver to be worn on Honorable Mention Award bar for 150 and more Honorable Mentions.
      NOTE:
      The bronze or silver oak leaf will be attached to the ribbon bar prior to shipment to the Chicago Police Department.
III.RIBBON BARS – INDIVIDUAL AWARDS

    Color: red and white. Centered will be a white stripe measuring approximately 7/16 inch wide. There shall be a red strip measuring approximately 7/16 inch wide on either side.

    Color: dark blue and white. Centered will be a white stripe measuring approximately 7/16 inch wide. There shall be a dark blue strip measuring approximately 7/16 inch wide on either side.

  • F.Superintendent's Award of Tactical Excellence

    Color: centered will be a 1/4 inch wide vertical black stripe. The right side (wearer's) will be medium green. The left side (wearer's) will be bluebird blue.
