I.UNIFORM - BDU, SWAT
  • A.DESIGN:
    The SWAT BDU Uniform is the 5.11 TDU uniform or equivalent and consists of:
    • 1.military-green BDU shirt and trouser.
    • 2.Department-approved tactical boots.
    • 3.BDU military-green seasonal winter jacket.
  • B.ACCESSORIES
    Accessory uniform items such as helmets, goggles, alternate holsters, ammunition case, gloves, and knee and elbow pads may be worn if approved by the Chief, Special Functions Division.
