Chicago Police DepartmentDepartment Notice D11-01
Online Case Reporting Pilot Program - Phase I
Issue Date:10 January 2011Effective Date:10 January 2011
Rescinds:D11-01
Index Category:Department Notice
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.informs Department members of the implementation of a pilot program for a new online case reporting system for public use.
  • B.explains the procedures and responsibilities of Department members in processing these online reports.
II.Scope
During this pilot program, only the following incidents may be reported online:
  • A.Theft, under $500 (IUCR code 0820, offender unknown).
  • B.Lost Property (IUCR code 5070).
III.General Information and Procedures
  • A.During the pilot program, incidents which may be reported are limited to theft (under $500) and lost property. In the case of theft, the offender must be unknown.
  • B.The application is available to the public at www.chicagopolice.org from the Chicago Police Department’s Online Reporting System link.
IV.Responsibilities
  • A.The Alternate Response Section will use beat numbers 9131-9139 when obtaining Records Division (RD) numbers for online reports and will:
    • 1.receive information for the reports via the online data collection system.
    • 2.determine the validity of the report.
    • 3.generate a Records Division (RD) number.
    • 4.enter the report in the CHRIS system.
    • 5.send the reporting person the Records Division (RD) number via email.
  • B.The Detective Division will receive the report from the Alternate Response Section through standard procedures. Any report received by the Detective Division with reporting beats of 9131-9139 will have originated in the online reporting system.
  • C.The Department will establish a committee which will meet, as needed, and make recommendations to continue, expand, modify, or terminate this pilot program. The committee will consist of the following members or their designees:
    • 1.Deputy Superintendent, Bureau of Patrol;
    • 2.Chief, Detective Division;
    • 3.Managing Deputy Director, Public Safety Information Technology;
    • 4.Director, Research and Development Division;
    • 5.Commanding Officer, Alternate Response Section.
