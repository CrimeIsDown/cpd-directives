Chicago Police DepartmentEmployee Resource E05-04-01
Academic Selection Board
Issue Date:17 June 2015Effective Date:17 June 2015
Rescinds:11 February 2015 Version
Index Category:Career Development
I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.introduces the Academic Selection Board.
  • B.continues the applicant evaluation and selection procedures for the participation of members in select management educational programs.
II.Policy
It is the policy of the Department to consider eligible sworn members for attendance at select management educational programs.
III.Academic Selection Board
  • A.The Academic Selection Board consists of the following members:
    • 1.First Deputy Superintendent
    • 2.Chief, Bureau of Patrol
    • 3.Chief, Office of Crime Control Strategies
    • 4.Chief, Bureau of Detectives
    • 5.Chief, Bureau of Organized Crime
    • 6.Chief, Bureau of Support Services
    • 7.Chief, Bureau of Internal Affairs.
  • B.The First Deputy Superintendent will serve as chairperson of the Academic Selection Board and set forth the selection procedure for the participation of members in select management educational programs.
  • C.The Chief, Office of Crime Control Strategies, will serve as vice-chairperson of the Academic Selection Board.
  • D.The Director, Human Resources Division, will serve as secretary to the Academic Selection Board as a non-voting member.
  • E.Academic Selection Board meetings will be conducted only with a quorum of the membership present. The presence of the following members constitute a quorum:
    • 1.The First Deputy Superintendent, as chairperson of the Academic Selection Board, or the Chief, Office of Crime Control Strategies, as vice-chairperson of the Academic Selection Board; and
    • 2.two other members of the Academic Selection Board.
  • F.It is the responsibility of the Academic Selection Board to:
    • 1.announce the availability of openings for select management educational programs.
    • 2.review the applications and make recommendations to the Superintendent of Police.
  • G.The Superintendent of Police will make the final determination for all selections.
  • H.Records of all Academic Selection Board applications will be retained in accordance with existing record-retention requirements.
  • I.Procedures delineated in the Department directive entitled "Travel, Department-Funded Training, and Reimbursement Guidelines" will be followed whenever expenses associated with registration fees, transportation, lodging cost, etc., are incurred by the Department.
IV.Select Management Educational Programs
  • A.School of Police Staff and Command of the Northwestern University Center for Public Safety
  • B.National Academy Program of the Federal Bureau of Investigation
  • C.Similar programs funded by the Department.
V.Application Process
  • A.Department supervisors who wish to attend select management educational programs will submit a Personnel Action Request (PAR) formset (CPD-11.612) to the Director, Human Resources Division, Attention: Chairperson, Academic Selection Board.
  • B.The PAR formset will be accompanied by a:
    • 1.resume, and
    • 2.letter of recommendation (in the form of a To-From-Subject report) from the member's commanding officer.
    NOTE:
    Applications not accompanied by the above documents will be returned as incomplete.
