Chicago Police DepartmentDepartment Notice D11-05
Specific Cost Recovery Pilot Program
Issue Date:27 July 2010Effective Date:27 July 2010
Rescinds:D10-18
Index Category:Department Notice
I.Purpose
This pilot program:
  • A.establishes procedures to recover cost relative to personnel and equipment use when an offender is charged with Aggravated Battery against a Chicago Police Officer.
  • B.introduces the use of the Cost Recovery Incident Form - Detective Division (CPD-61.231), available online.
II.Scope
This city-wide pilot program is effective 28 July 2010, 0000 hours.
III.General Information
Cost recovery incidents relative to this pilot program are only applicable to 720 5/12-4 “Aggravated Battery Against Protected Public Employees.”
IV.Responsibilities
  • A.The assigned Detective Division member will, in any case for which Aggravated Battery to a police officer has been approved by felony review will recommend this event for consideration as a personnel or equipment cost recovery incident by:
    • 1.completing a Cost Recovery Incident Form – Detective Division.
    • 2.attaching copies of the supporting documents (e.g., PCAD-printout for event, Arrest Report, case report, or a To-From-Subject Report to the member’s watch commander describing the incident).
    • 3.articulating in the Cost Recovery Incident Form – Detective Division information such as how many hours of duty were devoted to investigating this particular crime.
    • 4.submitting it through their chain of command to their appropriate command staff member for review and approval.
  • B.The Detective Division command staff member will forward the cost recovery package directly to the Director of Finance.
