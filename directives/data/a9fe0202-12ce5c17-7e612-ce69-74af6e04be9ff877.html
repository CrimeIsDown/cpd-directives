II.General Procedures
  • A.Property subject to the administrative disposal
    Disposal
    X
    Rendering the property unsuitable for use, offering the property for sale at auction, donating the property, or turning the property over to the Department or other City agency for use.
    procedure includes all property held by ERPS associated with an incident that is classified as:
    • 1.a felony or misdemeanor with no named offender and the statute of limitations for prosecution has lapsed.
    • 2.a felony or misdemeanor with a named offender, the court case has been adjudicated, and the appeal process has expired.
    • 3.“Found” or “Prisoner’s Personal Property” retained longer than 30 days.
    • 4.no property will be disposed of in violation of established retention schedules.
  • B.The Executive Officer, ERPS, will, in consultation with the Chief, Bureau of Administration:
    • 1.establish written unit-level procedures for the selection, review, and approval for the administrative disposal of property.
    • 2.approve, in writing, all administrative disposals of property.
