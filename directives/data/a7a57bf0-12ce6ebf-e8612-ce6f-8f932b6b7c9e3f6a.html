I.Purpose
This directive:
  • A.informs Department members of the following approved uniform items:
    • 1.Special Weapons and Tactics (SWAT) Program uniform.
    • 2.Battle Dress Uniform (BDU) Winter Jacket.
    • 3.Alternate three-season jackets.
    • 4.Alternate COBMEX sweater.
    • 5.Additional Alternate Blauer sweater.
    • 6.Search Warrant Duty Bag.
  • B.provides uniform specification numbers and approved manufacturers of the new uniform items.
II.Descriptions
  • A.ALTERNATE THREE-SEASON JACKET a jacket made of 100 percent Tactel Nylon manufactured by the following companies:
    • 1.Spiewak
    • 2.Newport Harbor
    • 3.Feccheimer Bros.
    • 4.Advance Uniform Co.
  • B.BDU JACKET - An alternate winter jacket worn with the tactical BDU uniform only. The BDU JACKET consists of the Spiewak Model S1780 Tactical Response Jacket or equivalent and has:
    • 1.a 100 percent nylon outer shell or equivalent military-approved tactical fabric, is hooded, hip-length, and black in color.
    • 2.an accessible side zipper with zip-out liner.
    • 3.optional hidden pull-down identification panels, front and back.
    • 4.muted black/gray patch and flag.
  • C.BLAUER SWEATER - An alternate duty V-neck sweater with fleece liner.
  • D.COBMEX SWEATER - An alternate duty V-neck sweater with optional liner.
  • E.SEARCH WARRANT DUTY BAG - A bag equipped with practical items used in the physical execution of a search warrant, such as arrest kits, flashlight, gloves, prybar, and inventory bags, with the exception of door-ramming devices. The search warrant duty bag will consist of:
    • 1.600 denier nylon carry-all, black in color;
    • 2.the following dimensions: 18.5 inches wide, 8 inches deep, and 12inches tall;
    • 3.baton loops and a zippered velcro top;
    • 4.an internal organizer;
    • 5.internal and external multi-pockets;
    • 6.hand and shoulder straps.
  • F.SWAT PROGRAM UNIFORM - The SWAT program uniform identifies members of the SWAT program. This uniform is technically designed to allow freedom of movement, comfort, and functionality for field-related duties. The SWAT PROGRAM UNIFORM is the 511 TDU uniform or equivalent and consists of:
    • 1.military-green BDU shirt and trouser;
    • 2.Department-approved tactical boots;
    • 3.BDU military-green seasonal winter jacket.
      NOTE:
      Accessory uniform items such as helmets, goggles, alternate holsters, ammunition case, gloves, and knee and elbow pads may be worn if approved by the Deputy Chief, Special Functions Group.
III.General Information
The following are the approved uniform items by specification and manufacturer:
  • A.SWAT Program Uniform - (9161.230) - 511 Tactical Series
  • B.BDU Winter Jacket - (9161.175) - Spiewak
  • C.Sweater, Alternate - (9161.231) - Cobmex
  • D.Sweater, Alternate - (9161.232) - Blauer
  • E.Search Warrant Bag - (9161.21) - 511 Tactical Series
  • F.Additional Three-Season Jackets- (9161.174) - Feccheimer Brothers Co., Advanced Uniforms, Spiewak, Newport Harbor
IV.Authorization and Restrictions
Department members who are authorized to wear the newly approved uniform items will:
  • A.adhere to current Department uniform standards.
  • B.refrain from any alterations that will change the basic fit and designed purpose of the uniform.
