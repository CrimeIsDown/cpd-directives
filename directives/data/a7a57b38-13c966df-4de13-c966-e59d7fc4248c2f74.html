Chicago Police DepartmentDepartment Notice 13-03
Office Supply - Ordering
Issue Date:01 February 2013Effective Date:01 February 2013
Rescinds:
Index Category:Department Notice
I.PURPOSE
This directive:
  • A.introduces the Department's new office supply vendor, Office Depot.
  • B.discontinues the use of Staples as an office supply vendor.
  • C.introduces a permanent ordering matrix for all units within the Department.
  • D.continues the use of the Material Requisition [CPD-34.622 (Rev.1/11)] for emergency supply and CPD form ordering only.
II.TRAINING
  • A.Unit commanding officers will schedule designated members for training when notified by the Finance Division.
  • B.Training in the use of the Office Depot/City of Chicago website for supply ordering will be conducted by Finance Division personnel and representatives from Office Depot.
  • C.Only members who receive proper training are authorized to use the Office Depot/City of Chicago website for placement of orders.
III.TRAINING MANUAL
The Office Depot Training Manual, provides step-by-step procedures for online account information, ordering, returns, and invoice processing via the Office Depot Business Services Division website.

NOTE:
All supply orders will be placed on one order, with the exceptions of paper and toner. Both paper and toner will be placed on their own individual separate orders. In addition, all orders must have a minimum cost of $50.00 for processing.
V.RESPONSIBILITIES/PROCEDURES
  • A.Each unit has designated through the Finance Division, at least one member as an initiator, the first person in the workflow for requesting the purchase of supplies.
  • B.A designated approver, usually the unit's commanding officer is responsible for viewing the request and approving or rejecting the requisition.

    • 3.select 'Order History.'
    • 4.use the search tool to find the order.
    • 5.select your order number to view the order detail.
    • 6.print out the order detail .
      NOTE:
      Finance Division will email each unit a FMPS receiving report. Each unit must log into FMPS and receipt the order, print the receipt number at the top of the order detail sheet, and the receiving report. Both documents will be returned via email to the Finance Division, Procurement Section.

VIII.EMERGENCY REQUESTS
All emergency supply requests will require immediate supervisory approval. In addition, the designated member will:
  • A.complete the Material Requisition form, then
  • B.scan, and email the document to the General Support Division, at generalsupport@chicagopolice.org for processing.
