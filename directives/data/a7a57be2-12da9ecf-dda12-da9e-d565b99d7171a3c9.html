Chicago Police DepartmentSpecial Order S04-21-05
Notification Procedures – Vandalism Incidents
Issue Date:26 August 1993Effective Date:27 August 1993
Rescinds:S93-15
Index Category:Preliminary Investigations
I.Purpose
This directive outlines notification procedures to be followed when effecting arrests for violations of Section 8-4-060 of the Municipal Code of Chicago (Vandalism).
II.Relevant Statutes
  • A.The Municipal Code of Chicago, Section 8-4-060
  • B.Section 8-4-070
III.Procedures
  • A.When the person arrested for violation of Section 8-4-060 MCC is an unemancipated minor (17-20 years of age, unmarried, living with a parent or legal guardian) the following will be required:
    • 1.The arresting officer will enter the following information in the narrative portion of the case report: the name, address and zip code of the parent or legal guardian with whom the arrestee is residing.
    • 2.Upon receipt of the appropriate case report, district review office personnel will:
      • a.enter the required information in the form letter entitled "Notification to Parent / Legal Guardian" (CPD-11.485)
      • b.insert the form letter into a certified mail envelope (CPD-34.306) and seal the envelope.
      • c.obtain a US Postal Service Form 3811 from any post office and enter the following information only:
        • (1)box three: name and address of parent/legal guardian.
        • (2)box four: check boxes marked certified and return receipt for merchandise.
        • (3)reverse side: name and address of sender.
      • d.affix US Postal Service Form 3811 to the back of the certified envelope and forward to the Police Document Services Section.
      • e.indicate on the district station copy of the appropriate case report that the required information was forwarded to the Police Document Services Section.
      • f.maintain a file of all returned envelopes, Post Office Notice Left - No Response (NLNR) forms and executed US Postal Service Form 3811 forms.
    • 3.The Police Document Services Section will:
      • a.assign a certified letter number to the US Postal Service Form 3811 and enter the information in the Firm Mailing Book.
      • b.deliver the certified envelopes to the Main Post Office daily, Monday through Friday.
      • c.forward returned envelopes, Post Office Notice Left - No Response (NLNR) forms and executed US Postal Service Form 3811 forms to the originating unit.
  • B.Arrest for vandalism under the provisions of this directive will be returnable to the appropriate local misdemeanor / ordinance court branch and court call in accordance with the Department directive entitled "Court Call Schedule."
  • C.Juvenile offenders (under 17 years of age) arrested for vandalism are excluded from the notification procedures set forth in this directive. The Department directive entitled "Processing of Juveniles and Minors Under Department Control" will be followed when processing juveniles arrested for vandalism.
