II.Promise to Comply
  • A.The following persons will have the option of signing the "Promise to Comply" section of the Personal Service Citation in lieu of bail for minor traffic violations as specified in 625 ILCS 5/6-306.4:
    • 1.Licensed motorists residing in the above listed jurisdictions, other than residents of Illinois. For residents of Illinois refer to Section III of the Department Directive entitled, "Bond Procedures."
    • 2.Members of the diplomatic community, regardless of residency, possessing a United States Department of State Drivers License.
  • B.A licensed motorist residing in Alaska, California, Michigan, Montana, Oregon, and Wisconsin will be required to post bail as provided in current Department directives.
III.Violations Excluded From Promise to Comply in Lieu of Bail
A non-resident issued a Personal Service Citation for violating provisions of the Illinois Vehicle Code will not be allowed the option of signing a Promise to Comply in lieu of bail for the following state provisions, or similar local ordinances, as specified in 625 ILCS 5/6-306.4:
