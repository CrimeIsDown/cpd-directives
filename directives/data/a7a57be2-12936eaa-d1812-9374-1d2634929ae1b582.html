I.Purpose
The following addenda provide procedures relative to police actions and investigations which may affect conduct protected by the First Amendment. Department members will refer to the General Order titled "The First Amendment and Police Actions" for the policy governing First Amendment investigations.
Garry F. McCarthy
Superintendent of Police
